ABOUT ME

I'm Valeria Martínez, a third-year graphic design student at UDIT in Madrid, Spain. Throughout my studies, I’ve had the chance to work on a range of projects, including branding, logo design, app design, and creating exhibitions. I have a strong passion for illustration and branding, as they allow me to bring ideas to life through visuals, creating unique and impactful visual languages.

Want to see my work? Feel free to check out the portfolio section!

I believe design is a powerful tool that can turn concepts into realities. In my free time, I enjoy practicing yoga, running, reading, and traveling.

SKILLS

Over the years, I have had the opportunity to work on a variety of projects at school. As a result, I have knowledge of tools such as Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign, and Figma, which allows me to tackle various design challenges with creativity and precision. For the future, I would like to develop advanced skills in motion graphics and animation to enhance storytelling in branding projects and enhance my abilities in creating strategic campaigns that integrate compelling visuals with impactful messaging. I’m also eager to learn more about digital marketing, particularly how branding aligns with platforms like social media, email campaigns, and content marketing.
